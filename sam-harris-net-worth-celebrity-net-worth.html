What is Sam Harris' Net Worth?

Sam Harris is an American author, neuroscientist, and philosopher who has a net worth of $12 million. Sam Harris was born in Los Angeles, California in April 1967. He is the co-founder of the non-profit organization Project Reason. Harris hosts the podcast Waking Up with Sam Harris. His book The End of Faith was published in 2004.

The book was featured on The New York Times Best Seller list for 33 weeks and won awards. Harris has also authored the books Letter to a Christian Nation in 2006, The Moral Landscape in 2010, Lying in 2011, Free Will in 2012, Waking Up: A Guide to Spirituality Without Religion in 2014, and Islam and the Future of Tolerance: A Dialogue in 2015. He is a critic of religion and prefers scientific skepticism and "new atheism". Harris has also written articles for Newsweek, The Huffington Post, The Washington Post, Los Angeles Times, and The New York Times. He graduated from Stanford University and the University of California, Los Angeles.
