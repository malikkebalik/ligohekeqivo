Who is Jenny Han?

Jenny Han is an American author who writes books for young adults and children. She is famous for creating two popular series: To All the Boys and The Summer I Turned Pretty. Both series were turned into movies and TV shows.

One of her earliest books is Shug, a children’s novel about a twelve-year-old girl named Annemarie who’s dealing with challenges in middle school.

Jenny started her writing journey at a young age. She loved writing even as a kid and continued through her teenage years. She knew she wanted to write or be involved with children’s books when she took a class in college.

After graduating, Jenny faced choices about her career but decided to follow her passion for writing. She went on to write successful novels like Shug and The Summer I Turned Pretty. The latter became a hit TV series on Amazon Prime.

Jenny Han has accomplished a lot in her writing career and continues to bring her stories to life on both the big and small screens.

Jenny Han Career

Jenny Han is a writer who started with a children’s book called Shug while she was in college. It was about a twelve-year-old girl named Annemarie dealing with challenges in junior high.

After that, she wrote a popular trilogy for young adults about a girl named Belly and her experiences during summer breaks. The books, titled The Summer I Turned Pretty, It’s Not Summer Without You, and We’ll Always Have Summer, became bestsellers.

Jenny Han also co-wrote another trilogy with Siobhan Vivian, starting with Burn for Burn in 2012. These books tell the story of three girls seeking revenge in their town, including paranormal and romance elements.

In 2014, Jenny Han released To All the Boys I’ve Loved Before, a romance novel about a high school student named Lara Jean whose life gets complicated when her secret love letters are sent out. The book was a hit and was turned into a successful Netflix film series.

Jenny Han’s storytelling expanded into television. In May 2023, a spinoff series called XO, Kitty from the To All the Boys films was released, focusing on Lara’s sister, Kitty Covey, and her quest for love.

In 2022, a TV series based on Jenny Han’s book series The Summer I Turned Pretty premiered on Amazon Prime. Notably, the show made changes for diversity, with the main character, Belly, being biracial. The series was renewed for a second season before the first season even came out.
