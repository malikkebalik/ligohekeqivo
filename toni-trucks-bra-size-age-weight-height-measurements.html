Biography:

Toni Trucks was born on September 30, 1980 in Grand Rapids, Michigan, U.S. She entered the prestigious Interlochen Arts Academy. She then attended University of Michigan where she earned her BFA in Musical Theatre.

She relocated to Los Angeles after being cast as the female lead in the acclaimed comedy series Barbershop, marking her television debut.

She also played the role of Cyndi Vega in the CBS legal drama Made in Jersey. Her film work includes Ruby Sparks, ‘Dreamgirls, ‘Weapons, Music and Lyrics and the fourth Twilight film, Breaking Dawn.
