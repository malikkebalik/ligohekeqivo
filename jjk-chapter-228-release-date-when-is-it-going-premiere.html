When is Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 228 Going To Release?

On July 10 at 12 AM JST, Shueisha’s Shonen Jump will publish Chapter 228 of Jujutsu Kaisen. The chapter is now accessible on the Manga Plus app for Android and iOS users.

  • Pacific Time: 7 AM, 9th July
  • Central Time: 9 AM, 9th July
  • Eastern Time: 10 PM, 9th July
  • British Time: 3 PM, 9th July
  • European Time: 4 PM, 9th July
  • India Time: 8:30 PM, 9th July
  • Japan Time: 12 AM, 10th July
  • Australia Time: 12:30 AM, 10th July

The English versions of Chapter 228 will be available on the official Manga Plus app and the Viz Media website virtually immediately after the first Japanese publication.

Predictions for Jujutsu Kaisen Chapter 228

The next episode of Jujutsu Kaisen will most likely go within the small Infinite Void. Readers have long hypothesized that the conditions inside this Infinite Void will be very different from those in the original Gojo’s Domain. Since Gojo’s Technique has always relied on both time and distance, it seems unnecessary to also factor in mass.

Nonetheless, Akutami, the manga artist, could opt to use the same approach in his own work. Condensing a Domain into a tiny ball could exponentially enhance its other factors, just as decreasing the volume of anything increases its density. Chapter 228 of Jujutsu Kaisen is likely to be another text-heavy and analytical issue since Akutami will clarify this problem in detail.

When reading Jujutsu Kaisen chapter 228, many readers assume the perspective to shift to Kenjaku, who is gone doing away with the survivors of the Culling Game. Since more than half of the people in the chamber where Yuta and the others are watching the combat are survivors from the Culling Game, Kenjaku should show up there in the next few chapters.
