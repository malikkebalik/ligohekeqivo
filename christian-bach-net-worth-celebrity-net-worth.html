What is Christian Bach's Net Worth?

Christian Bach is an Argentine-Mexican actress and producer who has a net worth of $20 million. Christian Bach was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina in May 1959. She graduated with a law degree and then moved to Mexico to become an actress. In many of her films her voice was dubbed to cover up her Argentine accent. Her first role came in the film Brigada en accion in 1977. She starred in TV series Te sigo queriendo Ana, La mujer frente al amor, Los ricos tambien lloran, Veronica, Propedad horizontal, Colorina, Soledad, Bodas de odio, El amor nunca muere, Encadenados, De pura sangre, Altrapada, Televiteatros, Bajo un mismo rostro, La antorcha encendida, La chacala, Agua y aceite, Vidas robadas, La Patrona, and La Impostora.

She has also produced two films and seven telenovelas. Christian recorded a solo album but it was not successful. She has been a long time spokesperson for Mexican ceramic tile company Interceramic. Christian married Mexican actor Humberto Zurita in 1986 and the couple has two sons together.
