Age, Biography and Wiki

Sean Connery is a Scottish actor and producer who has a net worth of $350 million. He is best known for his portrayal of the character James Bond in seven of the Bond films between 1962 and 1983. He is also known for his roles in films such as The Hunt for Red October, The Rock, Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade, The Untouchables, and Highlander. Connery was born on August 25, 1930 in Fountainbridge, Edinburgh, Scotland. He was raised in a working-class family and left school at the age of 13 to work as a milkman. He later joined the Royal Navy, but was discharged due to medical reasons. Connery began his acting career in the 1950s, appearing in small roles in British films. He gained international fame for his role as James Bond in the first Bond film, Dr. No, in 1962. He went on to appear in six more Bond films, and his portrayal of the character is considered to be one of the best. Connery has won numerous awards for his acting, including an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in The Untouchables. He has also been knighted by Queen Elizabeth II for his services to film drama.
