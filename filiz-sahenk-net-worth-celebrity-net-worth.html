What is Filiz Sahenk's Net Worth?

Filiz Sahenk is a Turkish business woman who has a net worth of $2.4 billion. Filiz Sahenk was born to a family who started one of the top three largest private-sector conglomerates in Turkey. Sahenk heads the luxury brand clothing and tourism businesses in her family's Dogus Holding. With a portfolio of 25 companies that cross industry verticals, Dogus Holding has interests in finance, media, tourism and construction companies, but still their main investment is in Turkey's largest bank, Garanti Bank.

Representing global brands like Tod's, Emporio Armani, Gucci, Loro Piana and In-Formal in Turkey, Filiz joined her brother Ferit Sahenk and mother Deniz Sahenk on the billionaire list. Furthermore, she is a great philanthropist and serves as the President of the Ayhan Sahenk Foundation, established in her father's name, which has been undertaking initiatives in education, health and the environment. According to reports, this highly successful middle-aged lady is still single.
