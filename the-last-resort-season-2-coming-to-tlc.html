'90 Day: The Last Resort' will return for a second season on TLC this winter.

Published on May 25, 2024

2 min read

In 2023, TLC launched another addition to the ever-expanding 90 Day Fiancé universe. 

90 Day: The Last Resort followed five of the franchise’s couples as they participated in a one-of-a-kind couples retreat. In a last-ditch attempt to save their troubled relationships, the cast members came together for a group retreat to hash out their issues and perhaps find a way forward in their marriages. 

The first season of The Last Resort wrapped up in late October 2023. Since then, 90 Day fans have been wondering if the show would return for another season and, if it did, which couples would be featured. Well, now we have answers to at least one of those questions. 

‘90 Day: The Last Resort’ Season 2 is coming to TLC

TLC has renewed 90 Day: The Last Resort for season 2. Parent company Warner Bros. Discovery confirmed that a second season was in the works during its upfront presentation on May 15. 90 Day: The Last Resort Season 1 was the top new cable show of 2023, according to the company. 

So far, details about 90 Day: The Last Resort Season 2 are scarce. However, we do know that it will take place in a new location. (Season 1 was filmed at the Isla Bella Beach Resort in the Florida Keys.) The new season will air sometime this winter. 

Which couples will be part of the ‘90 Day: The Last Resort’ Season 2 cast? 

TLC hasn’t revealed which couples will check in for season 2 of 90 Day: The Last Resort. But in March, several 90 Day stars were spotted filming in Arizona, leading to speculation that they were part of the season 2 cast, In Touch reported. 

The rumored 90 Day: The Last Resort Season 2 cast members include Sophie Sierra and Rob Warne; Brandon Gibbs and Julia Trubinka, Jasmine Pineda and Gino Palazzolo; and Ariela Weinberg and Biniyam Shibre. In late March, both Rob and Jasmine shared photos on social media that appeared to be taken at the same location, suggesting they were filming together for TLC. 

Season 1 of 90 Day: The Last Resort featured Angela Deem and Michael Ilesanmi; Yara Zaya and Jovi Dufren; Kalani Faagata and Asuelu Pulaa; Big Ed Brown and Liz Woods; and Molly Hopkins and Kelly Brown. Of the five couples who participated in season 1, only Yara and Jovi are still together. Molly and Kelly and Kalani and Asuelu broke up during filming. Ed and Liz reconciled but broke up again during a recent episode of 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After?, apparently for good this time. Angela and Michael also appear to be headed for divorce. 

90 Day: The Last Resort Season 1 is streaming on Max.
